Who We Are

EcoServe is a sustainable packaging supplier focused on replacing single-use plastic products with eco-friendly alternatives made from bagasse, areca leaf, paper and wood. Our mission is to support businesses in building a cleaner and greener future.

Our Vision

We envision a future where eco-friendly packaging becomes the standard choice for every business and event. Through innovation and responsible sourcing, we aim to contribute to a cleaner environment and a more sustainable economy.
